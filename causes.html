What are the causes of Influenza?

The most important thing for you guys to understand is that the flu is contagious. The flu spreads mostly from person to person through the coughs and sneezes of people who are already sick with the flu. You may also get sick by touching something with flu viruses on it, such as a table or doorknob and then touching your mouth or nose.

What are fomites?

Fomites can be any surface that carries germs, which also means that fomites are non-living. Fomites have the ability to carry infectious organisms such as viruses or bacteria and allow them to transfer from one individual to another. Some examples of fomites are tables, chairs, doorknobs, and sink handles.
