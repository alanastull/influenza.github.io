Hygiene

Maintain Health

  • Eat health food
  • Stay away from those who are sick or who have the flu, cold..etc
  • Make sure to clean up after yourself

Sanitation

  • Wash hands for 20 seconds
  • Wash dirty clothes and Furniture
  • Wash new clean clothes because the extra dye from the clothes can harm skin
  • Do not touch unsanitized objects

Public Health

  • Always cover your mouth when you sneeze or cough
  • Do not touch your face when you are in public areas
  • Put seat covers while using public toilets

Extra Facts

  • Wash your clothes because as you wear them they collect germs from anything around you
  • Wash hands everytime you eat,before touching your eyes, nose, or mouth, after using the bathroom and after touching doors, handsrails...etc.
